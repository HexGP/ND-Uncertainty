The Normal Deflection Field (NBField) predicts a quaternion (q0, q1, q2, q3) representing the rotation needed to align the SDF normal with the true surface normal.

6. Deflection Angle Guided Unbiased Rendering

Volume rendering with deflection angle guidance. Quaternions are composited along rays and used to compute biased normals. Alphas are computed using confidence-guided unbiased density estimation.
